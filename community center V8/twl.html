Club meetings:

We hold meetings every even numbered thursday

Hello and welcome to the white lodge, we are a group dedicated to helping the community of Dualberg thrive!
We love to help out around the community and make it a better place. A list of things that we do include:

  • cleaning trash from the street
  • Rescuing animals
  • Helping with groccery shopping
  • Driving people to appointments

As you can see we do a lot to help out the community and keep everyone
